    What Does KYC Mean?

    KYC refers to a regulatory process. "Know Your Customer" requires businesses, including casinos, to verify the identity of their customers. The reason behind this is the desire to deter illicit activities like money laundering and fraud. KYC verification in the gaming sector similarly ensures transparency and accountability.

    In the context of online gambling, KYC involves players providing personal information for verification purposes. Online gamblers must submit their personal information for verification before engaging in real-money gaming activities.5 The procedure typically includes documents such as government-issued IDs, proof of address, or even extra verification steps.

    The primary objective of KYC procedures in casinos is to establish the legitimacy of players and ensure compliance with legal and regulatory standards.
