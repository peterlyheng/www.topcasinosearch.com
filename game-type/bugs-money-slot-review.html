Join the fun with cute bugs and creepy critters in the new game Bugs Money from Reflex Gaming. Part of the YGS Masters series! In this game, bees make honey and also give you prizes. You can win a considerable amount - up to 55,028 times your bet - in this game about the beautiful world of bees and butterflies.
The Bugs Money game has five reels, three rows, and ten lines to win prizes on, which is a standard setup for online slots. This game gives you four types of free spins that can multiply your winnings by up to 50 times, so get ready for a fantastic ride. Bugs Money is a game for everyone. To win, you must get three or more of the same symbol in a row, starting from the first column.

Enter this fantastic world and try to win the favor of the queen bee for a chance to win big prizes. The game looks like it came straight out of a children's book, and the music matches the pictures to set a great mood. The Bugs Money game gives back 95.50% of the money players bet, a bit lower than the usual 96% for most games. 
The game does pay out quite often - about 29.6% of the time - so you'll get frequent wins. However, the prizes can be huge because it's a high-risk game. Start playing Bugs Money now and get a signup bonus from this Bugs Money game review!

How to Play Bugs Money Slot Game

  • Find a leading Reflex Gaming Casino.
  •  Customize the settings to your preferences.
  • Verify the game's rules and other details.
  • Select a bet from €0.20 to €100.

Game Symbols

  • Wild Symbol: There's a closed, empty bottle that acts like a wild card. It can replace any symbol on the wheel, except the Scatter symbol and the Queen Bee, to help you win more often. 
  • Scatter Symbol: The word "SCATTER" in a fancy frame is the scatter symbol in this game. You get some free spins when you see it three or more times anywhere on the wheel. 
  • Other Symbols: Symbols like the playing card suits of clubs, diamonds, hearts, and spades aren't worth as much. They come in different colors, like strawberries, beeswax, and more. If you get five of these, you get five times your bet. Symbols like blue flowers, butterflies, ladybirds, and purple butterflies are worth more. You can get between 20 to 100 times your bet if you get five of the same kind.

Bugs Money Game Bonus Features

You'll love playing the Bugs Money video slot because its unique features make it look and feel great. Here's how to use the game's extra bonuses and win big.

Glow Wilds Feature

You could suddenly get the Glow Wilds feature during any spin in the standard game or free spins. This means glow bugs, which are wild symbols, are added to the game. So, the more wild symbols you have, the better your chances are of winning a lot of money.

Free Spins Feature

Land 3 or more Scatter symbols to activate the Free Spins feature and rewards a bonus win of 8x the initial bet (3 Scatters), 40x the initial bet (4 Scatters), and 200x the initial bet (5 Scatters).

Win multipliers are given on each free spin, ranging from x1 to x50, depending on the option selected below.

Free Spins are available in 4 different ways:

➢ 3 free spins featuring a multiplier of 5x, 10x, 20x, or 50x.

➢ 7 free spins featuring a multiplier of 3x, 5x, 10x, or 20x.

➢ 10 free spins, each with a 2x, 3x, 5x, or 10x multiplier.

➢ 15 free spins featuring multipliers of 1x, 2x, 3x, and 5x.

The bonus and the selected amount of free spins are awarded when three scatter symbols land in the same position during the free spins round. There is no bonus win for three or more Scatters during Free Spins.

Only three free spins can be retriggered at most. Scatters only emerge on reels 1, 3, and 5 during free spins. The win multiplier gets boosted by x10 (for example, a multiplier of x50 increases to x500) if the Queen Bee symbol appears on reel three during the free spins. Free Spins can be added together.

Golden Bet

A player can double their chances of getting the Free Spins bonus by increasing the wager by 50%. Wins are paid depending on the initial stake and under the pay table.

Mobile Compatibility

Bugs Money is a modern game you can play on any size screen, even on your phone. It's made to work well with the touch controls you usually find on mobile devices. This means you can have fun with the game wherever you go, and the game will still work great. The creators thought about how well Bugs Money would work on mobile devices during design. So, whether you're playing on your computer or your phone, you'll have a fun and exciting gaming experience.

This is a great reason to play the game, even though it gives back less money to players (95.5%) compared to some other games. The game looks and sounds really good. The animations and music go together nicely to make playing the game enjoyable. Ready to play?
