    A Closer Look at the US Dollar

    The United States Dollar (USD) is the official currency of the United States. It is widely used for various financial transactions and is signified by "$."

    It has a historical background dating back to the late 18th century. This includes the transition to paper currency and the abandonment of the gold standard in 1971. Today, the USD is a fiat currency with global significance. It serves as a primary reserve currency and a preferred medium of exchange in international trade and finance.
