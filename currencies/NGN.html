    A Brief Overview of NGN: Online Casino Currency Insights 

    NGN, which stands for Nigerian Naira, is the official currency of Nigeria. It is used only within the borders of Nigeria and is the legal tender for all transactions in the country. The use of NGN began in 1973. This was shortly after Nigeria gained independence from British colonial rule in 1960. 

    The Nigerian Naira replaced the British West African pound that was in circulation during the colonial era. Since then, NGN has been Nigeria's primary currency for trade, commerce, and everyday financial transactions.
