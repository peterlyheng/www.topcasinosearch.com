Belize Online Gambling Laws, Regulations, And Rules

In Belize, gambling online is legal but regulated differently than in other countries. The following sections will guide you through the legal situation regarding Belize casinos online.

Are Online Gambling Sites Legal in Belize?

Online gambling websites are legal in Belize. However, the regulatory framework is not as clear-cut as in some other places. Belize grants licenses to online casino operators. It is legal to play on licensed casino sites. It's also essential to note that the legal framework primarily addresses land-based casinos and gaming establishments.

The Gambling Prevention Act covers unlicensed gambling activities. The Gaming Control Act covers licensed gambling facilities.

Belize Computer Wagering Licensing Act

The Belize Computer Wagering Licensing Act was enacted in 1995. It governs online betting by providing a licensing system for operators. It allows various online betting services, such as sports betting and casino games, to be offered.

In 2004, Belize updated its online gambling laws. They reduced the licensing fee from BZ$ 100,000 to BZ$ 30,000 (equal to US$ 15,000). They also required licensed Belizean online casinos to have customer support centers in Belize.

One advantage for casino operators is that once they get a Belizean online gambling license, they can host their servers anywhere. But these local casinos can't serve Belizean residents.

The Belize Computer Wagering Licensing Board sets the standards for getting a license.

Despite these laws, Belizean players can still enjoy gambling online on international online casinos. These often offer better quality. No law also stops Belizean players from accessing legal online casinos in other countries, giving them many reputable options.

Legal gambling age

To participate in any form of gambling, including online betting, you must be at least 18.
