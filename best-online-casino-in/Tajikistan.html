A Guide To Tajikistan Online Gambling Laws

Before playing at Tajikistan online casino sites, you should know the laws. The legislation on online gambling in Tajikistan is strict. Online gambling laws have taken different turns in Tajikistan's history. However, despite the strictness, there are certain loopholes. We are going to consider every aspect in this section.

Are Online Casinos Legal in Tajikistan?

No, online gaming sites are not legal in Tajikistan.

The Civil Code regulates online gambling in Tajikistan. Some other state laws also regulate it, including the Tajikistan law on Licensing of Separate Types of Activity. Due to the nature of the legislation, there is also no land-based or online gambling authority in Tajikistan.

The laws that guide gambling are pretty strict compared to other countries. Gambling used to be a legal and popular form of entertainment in the country, but it is now prohibited. It is illegal to transfer money from Tajikistan to any other country for online gambling activities. Gambling-related activities, like online sports betting, are also heavily fined. Despite being banned by the law, some illegal forms of gambling still take place. The citizens secretly participate in online sports betting, and rumors say some underground casinos exist. 

Thus, online casino sites are not legal in Tajikistan. But if you still wish to enjoy gambling, you can access foreign sites from other jurisdictions. This is one way to circumvent the law. 

What is the Tajikistan Legal Age Limit?

Every form of online gambling is prohibited in Tajikistan, including poker and sports betting. So, there's no age limit stipulated for online gambling. But, if you decide to play at offshore gambling sites, some have licenses from agencies that regulate the age limit of their users. In such cases, the age limit for the casino will be applied to Tajikistan players. The popular licensing agencies are U.K.G.C. and M.G.A. These agencies have an age limit of 18 years, which could influence the age limit of Tajikistan casinos.

Does Tajikistan Collect Taxes on Online Casino Winnings?

No, they don't. Online gambling is prohibited in Tajikistan, so no laws tax operators and players. The law doesn't recognize illegal casino operators and players, so the government doesn't tax them.

Local Casinos in Tajikistan

Although they have a history, local casinos no longer legally exist in Tajikistan. The government has taken strict measures to ensure land-based casinos don't exist legally. Apart from legislation, steps to enforce the law have also been taken, including shutting down discovered casinos and prosecuting offenders. 

Despite this, casinos are still crucial to the country's culture. There is a history of the nomadic tribes of Tajikistan playing and enjoying gambling games in the region. They even had a unique cultural gambling game called "Tog" or "Togo." This game involved rolling a dice and betting on the roll's outcome. Similar to the popular Craps game. It was well-known as a form of entertainment and money-making. There are still underground casinos and gaming venues that offer this game as entertainment for citizens, although it is now illegal.
