    History of Gambling in the North Macedonia

    Gambling used to be illegal in Macedonia when it was under the USSR. However, this changed in 1991 when gambling became legal and licensed. Gambling has various forms in the country's history, such as dice games and betting on sports and animal fights.

    The first casino opened its doors in the late 20th century and offered a range of casino games to Macedonians and visitors alike. Over the years, the number of casinos in the country has increased. Online gambling was introduced as the internet became popular.

    Macedonia began to take steps to make online gambling legal in 2011. It was finally made completely legal in 2014 when the first licenses were given to independent operators. Many offshore casinos that did not comply with the country's legislation were also blocklisted in 2014.

    Today, online betting is well-regulated in the country. While traditional forms of betting still exist, online casino sites have expanded the options for players. It has provided convenient access to a wide range of gaming experiences.
