Online Gambling Laws and Regulations in Vietnam

Vietnamese online gambling laws are strict and complex. On the one hand, there is the prohibition of gambling. On the other hand, exceptions are made for tourists and certain casinos. This section will shed more light on the legalities of gambling online in Vietnam.

Are Online Casinos Legal in Vietnam?

Generally, gambling is illegal in Vietnam. This includes traditional land-based gambling and online casinos. The Penal Code criminalizes gambling and prescribes fines and imprisonment for violators. Fines for participants in gambling can be as much as millions of Vietnamese Dong, while operators face more harsh punishment.

The government actively monitors internet activity to block access to any online casino. Some agencies also have the duty to shut down illegal gambling operations. These are government measures taken to prevent gambling, apart from the legislation.

There are exceptions to these laws. Some land-based casinos are allowed to operate. However, they are only limited to foreign tourists and expatriates. Local gamblers are banned from entering these establishments, except with certain conditions.

Despite restrictions, the government runs a state-run lottery and sports betting for Vietnamese players. Sports betting is limited to football, horse racing, and greyhound racing. Although sports bettors are strictly regulated, they serve as alternatives for gambling in Vietnam.

How Does Taxation Work in Vietnam

State-run lotteries and sports betting are the only legal gambling activities in Vietnam. This means they are the only form of gambling subject to taxation.

Revenue generated from state-run lotteries is subject to tax. However, the taxes vary based on the type of lottery games offered and the revenue made. Traditional number-based lotteries and instant-win scratch cards have different tax rates, ranging from a fixed percentage to fixed and variable rates. This also applies to Sports Betting activities.

Lastly, tax is collected by agencies that oversee gambling activities in the country. The revenue made from the tax is pushed into public services and projects.

The Future of Gambling in Vietnam

Gambling in Vietnam in the near future will be determined by regulatory development and social factors. Although the current legislation depicts otherwise, the country is showing acceptance of the gambling industry. This has begun with the Pilot Program.

The Vietnamese government launched programs that allow Vietnamese citizens to gamble in certain casinos, although they are still under strict regulations. This is a cautious social test to assess the impact of legal gambling on citizens. Selected land-based casinos, such as Corona Resort & Casino in Phu Quoc Island, offer these services. 

The success of this social test will determine whether any online casino will be accessible to Vietnamese citizens in the future. Also, since sports betting is legal in Vietnam, it is safe to expect other forms of betting to be legalized.

The government may expand Sports betting beyond Football and Horse racing. With the rise of offshore gambling, the Vietnamese government may also seek to regulate it. Frequently Asked Questions

What is the legal age for gambling in Vietnam?

The legal age for gambling in Vietnam is 21 years old. This is among the many other requirements for legal gambling at land-based casinos. 

What body regulates gambling in Vietnam?

The Ministry of Finance, Public Security, and the Office of the Prime Minister regulate gambling. Alongside other authorities, these three bodies regulate gambling and prevent criminal betting activities.

Is offshore gambling legal in Vietnam?

Vietnamese players can access online casinos through offshore sites. Although no law prohibits this, we advise players to be careful and avoid being detected.
