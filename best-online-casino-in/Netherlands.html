Online Poker in the Netherlands

Under the Remote Gambling Act, Dutch people can choose to play poker. Dutch players love playing poker games in tournaments and other forms of video poker. There are numerous types of online poker games. Some of the most famous examples are: 

Texas Hold 'em

Texas Hold 'em is the most popular poker game in the world. Casino players from all over the world enjoy it, Dutch players included.

The game plays out as follows:

  • Pre-flop – The dealer deals two 'hole' cards facing down for each player.
  • The flop – The dealer deals three 'community' cards facing up.
  • The turn – Fourth community card is being dealt.
  • The river – Fifth community card is dealt.

After each step, you can call, fold or raise based on the best hand you can make from the cards you have. The purpose is to have the best hand available from the combination of all the cards on the table. Based on the community cards, you can guess what the other players may have, and if you think you have a better hand, you can follow up with the bet. Otherwise, it may be best to fold. 

Omaha

Much like Texas Hold 'em, Omana is different because you receive 4 'hole' cards instead of two. You can only use two of them, along with the community cards. Five community cards are dealt similarly to Texas Hold 'em with a flop, turn, and a river card.

Seven-card stud

Before Texas Hold 'em took the poker world by storm, this game was the most popular one. Two to eight players can play this game. There are no community cards; each player receives three cards: two face-down cards and one face-up card.

Overall, each player receives seven cards; three are facing down, and four are facing up. With these cards, each player creates the best hand combination possible. 

The player with the best hand will win.

On this page, you will find online casinos with several blackjack variations. If you already know the ins and outs of the game, go ahead and choose any of the online casinos listed on this page.

If you're new to the blackjack world, let us make things simpler for you. 

The game begins after every player receives their set of two cards each, and at every turn, a player can either choose to draw additional cards or not. The game's ultimate goal is for a player to achieve a total score of 21, wherein each card is equivalent to its numerical value. 

Image cards in the deck, such as King, Queen, etc., are equivalent to a value of 10, and the ace card is equal to either 1 or 11. If you pass the value of 21, then you lose the game. You win if your score is below 21 and the dealer's score is above 21. Or if you have a total hand value closer to 21 than the dealer's hand.

Online Gambling Laws and Regulations in Netherlands

Online gambling is legal in the Netherlands. To operate within the country's laws, online casinos that accept players from the Netherlands must have a license from the Netherlands Gambling Authority (Kansspelautoriteit or KSA in short).

However, it is also not illegal for players to play in online casinos licensed by other countries. For example, you can play in online casinos licensed by the United Kingdom Gambling Commission (UKGC) or the Malta Gambling Authority (MGA). These two gambling authorities are considered highly reputable and safe. And online casinos with permits by these bodies are also highly recommended.

Having regulated online casinos in the Netherlands wasn't always the case. Until 2021, you could only play in UKGC, MGA, or other online casinos. There were no regulations regarding online casinos in the Netherlands. So, what changed?

With an understanding of players' needs and their large numbers, the country's government created the Netherlands Gambling Authority (KSA). The KSA is the regulating body that oversees all gambling activities in the country, including : 

  • Casino games
  • Sports betting
  • Live tables

The KSA receives its official power from the Remote Gambling Act, the law that states the country's official standards and gives power and a firm grip over gambling activities.

It allows foreign online casinos, which are approved by it, to operate under a specific set of rules. These rules help make the experience safer for the dutch people. They also prevent money laundering and other types of illegal actions.

Today, only a small number of active permits are provided by the KSA. And still, hundreds of online casinos accept players from the Netherlands. The government is not acting against these casinos and is OK with it. What matters most to the government is that there isn't an active effort to attract Dutch players via various forms of advertising to the dutch people.

This means that many of the world's leading online casinos accept Dutch players, although they don't have a license, and it's perfectly OK. 

On this page, we recommend both KSA-approved and non-KSA-approved casinos. The reason is simple. We want you to have the best possible experience. And many online casinos give a better for your money without compromising the quality of the gaming experience you receive.
