Is Online Gambling Legal in Kyrgyzstan?

Yes, online gambling is legal in Kyrgyzstan, but it is only limited to foreigners and tourists. The country issues gambling licenses to operators, but only foreigners and specific tourists can play. The country's citizens are not allowed to participate in any form of online gambling.

After Kyrgyzstan's independence, the country saw a rise in casino outlets. This led to the banning of all forms of gambling in the country in 2012, only allowing it in designated areas. This move was similar to that which Russia had made years prior. But while Russia already decided on the 4 specific areas for casinos to open, Kyrgyzstan remained undecided. This was not a welcome development, and people criticized their lack of decision, which shows they were unprepared.

Due to poor enforcement methods and corruption, the law failed. The number of casinos even increased within the months that the ban lasted. In 2014, the government proposed to legalize gambling again. The argument was that the country's economy was too weak to ignore the revenue that gambling would bring in. Later, in April 2021, Kyrgyzstan legalized gambling again. The legalization applied to both online and land-based casinos. However, the law restricts gambling activities to foreign visitors and tourists only.

The legal age for online gambling in Kyrgyzstan only applies to foreigners, and it is 21 years.
