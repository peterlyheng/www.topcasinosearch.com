Online Blackjack in Guernsey

Blackjack, or 21, is a classic casino card game. The basics of this game are reaching a total score of 21 by drawing more cards. If you pass 21, you go bust and lose your bet. If you get exactly 21, that's best.

The way the game works is that the dealer starts dealing with the cards, and each player around the table receives two cards. The dealer also receives two cards, one facing down.

Clockwise, each player gets to draw or sit, meaning to keep the total score of the hand he is holding. 

Ultimately the dealer unveils his hidden card and has to draw or hold based on specific rules rather than his judgment like players do.
