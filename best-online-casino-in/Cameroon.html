Is Online Gambling Legal in Cameroon?

Online gambling is in a bit of a grey area in Cameroon. While there aren't specific laws permitting or prohibiting it, the government generally allows online casinos to operate.

Online gambling in Cameroon was technically made legal in 2019 following the Décret N°2019/2300/PM. Only licensed gambling websites accept players from Cameroon.

Nationale Loterie Du Cameroun sells lottery tickets online. This shows that online gambling is legal in Cameroon. And that it is possible to operate domestic Cameroonian online gambling platforms legally. Players in Cameroon have access to international online casinos.

What is the Cameroon Legal Gambling Age?

The online gambling legal age in Cameroon is 18 years old. To partake in any form of gambling, including online casinos, you must be at least 18.

Are Taxes Collected on Online Casino Winnings in Cameroon?

Currently, Cameroon does not impose taxes on online casino winnings. This means you do not have to pay taxes if you win money while playing at an online casino. However, staying updated on any potential changes in tax regulations is essential.
