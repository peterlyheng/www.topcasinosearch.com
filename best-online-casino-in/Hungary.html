Online Blackjack in Hungary

Online Blackjack in Hungary is very prominent. The classic card game has many fans from around the globe. Online casinos listed on this page will engage you in blackjack for many hours, with a large selection of games.

Blackjack is a card game played against the dealer. Players sit around the table, and each player receives two cards. The dealer is the one to receive the last card. All players have their cards facing up, while the dealer has one card facing down.

Once every player receives their two cards, the game starts, and each player, in their turn, can choose to draw additional cards or not.

The goal is to achieve a total score of 21, with each card equal to its numeric value. And image cards (King, Queen, etc.) worth 10. The ace is worth 1, or 11. If you pass 21, you go bust and lose.

You win if you have less than 21, and the dealer goes above 21, or holds when his score is less than yours. And generally, you aim to score as high as possible without going bust. 

The dealer must also draw or hold based on specific rules. And he can't have any judgment in the matter.

Online Gambling Laws and Regulations in Hungary

Online gambling is legal in Hungary. Like most European countries, Hungary's government has passed several laws regulating the land-based and online industries. The current gambling law, Act No.34, and Concession Law, Act No.16, have strict terms upon which gambling platforms can operate.

The land-based casinos have been legal since 1991. The control and supervision of land-based casinos are the responsibility of state-owned Szerencsejáték Zrt. It regulates casino games, lottery, sports betting, and all other forms of land-based and online gambling activities. 

The online gambling industry was first made legal in 2013, and ever since, many gambling operators have joined the market. The large amount of online gambling operators operating in Hungary results in the Hungarian players having sweet deals.

An online gambling operator that wants to operate within the borders of Hungary must have a valid Hungarian license and pay taxes on revenues. They also must have this license renewed every five years.

The legal gambling age in Hungary is 18. You must provide your documents upon registration to any legal online casino in Hungary, and all the best online casinos in Hungary are legal ones. 

Online Gambling History in Hungary

Gambling in Hungary has always been popular, and historical records show that these games were already taking place back in the 18th century. Of course, during these years, it has also always been a hugely profitable industry. Starting as a small-time casino in Budapest in 1986, it has become one of Europe's top gaming destinations. Here we'll go through the story of how it got that way.

The first official gambling regulation in Hungary was set in around the 1750s by Maria Teresa, the queen of Habsburg. Maria Teresa created This regulatory act to regulate the Lottery games, which were very popular then.
As time went by, and during the 19th century, other types of gambling games became prevalent in the country. Among them are Poker, Blackjack, and also sports betting. Land-based casinos started to emerge on Hungary's soil when there started to be demand for these games. 

The first National state-owned casino was launched in 1827.
Since then, the country has held a firm grip and regulates all land-based casinos. The most popular ones are Tropicana, Casino Sopron, and the Las Vegas Casino in Budapest.
During the last decade, online casinos have been operating in Hungary under the country's gambling laws, which were amended in 2013. The primary condition that made it harder for operators to work freely in the country is that you must also have a land-based gambling establishment to receive a permit from the Hungarian government.
This law is still available today. 

Having said that, many online casinos that don't have a permit from the Hungarian government are operating in Hungary without problems. The country keeps a blind eye, and no citizen is ever prosecuted for gambling at short gambling websites.
