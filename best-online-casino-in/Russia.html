Is Gambling Legal in Russia?

Russian gambling regulation is complex, and not all forms of gambling are legal.

What Forms of Gambling Are Legal in Russia?

Russian legal gambling market comprises three sectors:

  1. state lotteries
  2. casino and electronic gambling machines
  3. online and offline bookmakers

All the other forms of online gambling are ILLEGAL.

Read this recent report to learn more about the gambling landscape in Russia.

Is Online Gambling Legal in Russia?

No. Online Gambling in Russia is ILLEGAL. The only LEGAL forms of online gambling are: 

  • Online sports betting
  • National lottery online.

Russia engages in IP blocking and blacklists websites. The latter includes European giants like Ladbrokes, Betfair and Unibet. More than 76K domains were blocked in 2018. Draft laws prescribe penalties for gambling on unlicensed websites and payment services alike. The rules apply to Russian citizens. The fines for individuals range from ₽2,000 to ₽2,500.

However, there’s no enforcement. What is more, many Russia-licensed online betting shops offer online casino games via their “shell” companies. If you click on a game on a sportsbook website, you’ll be redirected to their offshore counterpart. Such platforms will typically have international gambling licenses.

Is Land-Based Gambling Allowed in Russia

Yes, but with certain restrictions, as explained below. Brick-and-mortar gambling has been LEGAL since 1987.

“Special Gambling Zones” in Russia

Casino houses are allowed only in the defined gambling Zones:

  • Kaliningrad
  • Altai
  • Primorye
  • Krasnodar territory

The Crimean Peninsula should have been the first zone. But the political situation dragged the process.

Who Can Organize Gambling Activities in Russia?

First comes the state. Ministries organize lotteries via public companies and private distributors. Licensed private companies manage casinos and electronic gambling machines. These companies can be Russian or foreign. Private companies organize sports betting, too. They can either be Russian or foreign. The foreign ones must set up a legal entity in Russia to engage in this activity.

Lotteries in Russia

Since 2014, the state has been banning all private lotteries. Only the state-owned lotteries can participate in the market

2013 amendment to the 2003 law "On lotteries"

Who is in charge of lotteries?

Two ministries:

  1. Sportloto - The Ministry of Finance
  2. GSL - Gosudarstvennye sportivnye loterei, State Sports Lotteries - Ministry of Sports

Sports Betting in Russia

The Federal Tax Service issues licenses for sports betting establishments.

Russia saw the first online sports betting operators in 2016.

The complex registration process involves two steps:

  1. registration at the bookmaker-affiliated TSUPIS system (Payment Processing System for Online Gambling Transactions)
  2. registration with the betting website

In praxis, that meant that the players entered the same information twice.

It's no surprise that the cumbersome procedure pushed Russian players to unlicensed operators.

There are currently 29 licensed bookmaking companies.

Do I Have to Pay Taxes on My Gambling Winnings in Russia?

Income and expenses from gambling activities are tax-exempted. You do have to pay personal income tax, which is usually taken at the source. The tax rate is 13%. The taxation applies to all residents in Russia, not just the Russian citizens.
