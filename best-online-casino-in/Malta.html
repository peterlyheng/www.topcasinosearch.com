Malta's Gambling Laws and Regulators: A Comprehensive Guide

The Malta Gaming Authority (MGA) is the primary regulator of gambling in Malta. The MGA regulates all gaming activities in Malta, including Malta online casinos.

The MGA has a very liberal approach to online gambling and has issued licenses to many online gambling operators. It has made Malta a beautiful destination for online casinos and gambling companies.

The MGA has also introduced several player protection measures, such as mandatory KYC checks for all players and a self-exclusion system for problem gamblers.

The MGA is widely considered one of the world's most stringent and effective gambling regulators.

The registration process for an MGA license is simple. Any operators wishing to obtain a permit must visit the MGA website and enter the Licensee Relationship System. Once they've logged into the system, they can begin the official application process to become one of the casino sites in Malta.

It consists of 11 steps. The applicant must supply information about financial management, policies and procedures, gaming information, and other topics during the process. After submitting their application, The applicant may monitor the progression of their application in real-time. 

The Regulatory Directorate is reviewing the application. A permit will be issued if the application is approved and the operator can begin operations in Malta.
