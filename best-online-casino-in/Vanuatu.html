Online Gambling in Vanuatu: Legal or Not?

Online gambling is legal in Vanuatu. The government here controls and licenses online gambling operators. These companies must follow specific rules to offer their services legally. Vanuatu gamblers need to pick casinos that have licenses and a good reputation for a safe gaming experience.

The main laws that deal with gambling in Vanuatu are the Interactive Gaming Act of 2000, the Casino Control Act of 1993, and the Lotteries Act of 1989.

Vanuatu was one of the first places to regulate online betting. They're working hard to be a trusted source for gaming licenses worldwide. The Vanuatu Gaming Control Board (VGCB) handles the licensing of all online gambling facilities in the country.

What Age Do You Need to Be to Gamble in Vanuatu?
