Online Gambling Laws and Regulations in San Marino

In this section, we explore the legal aspects of online gambling in San Marino. We address questions about the legality of online casinos, the minimum age for gambling, and how gambling winnings are taxed.

Is Online Gambling Legal in San Marino?

Gambling online in San Marino is Legal! Even though it lacks official oversight, people in San Marino are allowed to bet on foreign websites.

Gambling in San Marino was made legal in 2007 through a set of laws -- mainly Law No.67 of 25 July 2000, Law No.143 of 27 December 2006, and Law No. 126 of 29 July 2014. However, online gambling sites are not yet regulated. The Ente di Stato dei Giochi (ESG) is the authority responsible for overseeing Land-based gambling activities and handling tax matters related to gambling in San Marino.

What is the Legal gambling age in San Marino?

According to San Marino's gambling laws, you must be at least 18 years of age to participate in gambling activities.

Are there Taxes on Online Gambling Winnings in San Marino?

Currently, San Marino does not have specific rules regarding taxes on online gambling winnings for players.
