Is Gambling Legal in Mozambique?

Gambling has been LEGALin Mozambique since 1994.

In the olden days, the strict rules made gambling unattractive enterprise.

For instance, casinos had to be outside of cities with a basic investment of 15M USD. On top of that, only 250+ room hotels could host casinos.

Over time, the movement towards a more business-friendly environment gained traction. Several amendments led to the present state of affairs. The rationale behind this was to stimulate the growth of the tourism and hospitality sectors.

After the legalization in 1994 (Decree 8/94), there have been three key liberalization moves up to date:

  1. Decree 64/2010
  2. Decree 4/2017
  3. Decree 65/2022

Find out more about gambling laws in Mozambique here.


 

Is Online Gambling Legal in Mozambique?

Yes, online gambling is LEGAL in Mozambique. Ever since the 2010 revision of the Gambling Act, local players can gamble at online casinos.

However, players from Mozambique are NOT to play in unlicensed online casinos. Playing on such operators is ILLEGAL.

Who Can Accept Players from Mozambique?

Licensed operators with the Mozambican gambling license. Only Mozambican gambling platforms with locally issued licenses can accept players.

Licensing is the jurisdiction of the Minister of Tourism.

Is It Legal to Play Offshore Gambling Platforms in Mozambique?

Only if they procure a local gambling license. To do so, they must set up shop in Mozambique. This ban applies to Mozambicans only.

Can Tourists Gamble Online from Mozambique?

Yes. The gambling regulations apply to Mozambicans. If casinos accept them, the non-permanent residents, tourists and expats can play online.

Who Regulates Gambling in Mozambique?

The regulation is a tad more complicated than elsewhere.

First, the Mozambican gambling market falls under two ministries:

  1. Ministry of Finance
  2. Ministry of Tourism

Next, two bodies regulate the market.

The General Gaming Inspection (IGJ) supervises the operation of all gambling and social games. In so doing, they assist the Finance Minister.

The National Gaming Commission evaluates proposals, awards contracts and issues licenses. They aid the Tourism Minister.

Basically, the Finance Minister okays casinos, while the Gaming Inspection licenses them.

What is the Legal Gambling Age in Mozambique?

Mozambican players can gamble from 18 years of age.

Bitcoin Casinos in Mozambique - Is Crypto Gambling Regulated in Mozambique?

Not really. Still, as cryptocurrencies are illegal, so is crypto gambling. No local gambling house accepts crypto deposits.

Players depositing on international gambling platforms won't suffer consequences. On this matter, the state's enforcement is lenient.

Using cryptocurrency may be risky, according to the National Monetary Authority. The local central bank warns against Bitcoin, as the currency has no legal standing. Subsequently, claiming damages that rise from transactions using cryptocurrencies may be tricky.

The application process for licenses is swift. The licenses are easily renewed. Looking at the history, only one conclusion emerges.
