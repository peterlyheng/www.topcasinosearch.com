Online casinos are affected by several factors. These factors affect their payment processing time and may cause players frustration. So, players need to recognize the factors and tackle them before withdrawing.

Wagering Requirements

This is the number of times a player must bet their deposit before they can withdraw bonus winnings at a casino. They are also called playthrough requirements. The higher the bonus, the more the wagering requirement. Bonus withdrawals usually have a 25x to 50x playthrough requirement. Meanwhile, a no-bonus withdrawal requires a 1x playthrough.

KYC Verification

Before players are allowed to withdraw at a casino, they must complete KYC verification. KYC verification is the method by which a player verifies their identity. The casino asks for ID documents and registers them. It is an anti-fraud or money laundering regulation. We advise players to complete this process before they are ready to withdraw at the casino. This will help prevent unnecessary delays in getting winnings.

Withdrawal limits

Every casino has a withdrawal limit. It is the highest amount a player is allowed to withdraw within a period. The period for withdrawal may be in a day, week, or month. Once a player exceeds this limit, they won't withdraw until the time ends. We prefer casinos with a high or no withdrawal limit. This frustrates players less and generally improves the gaming experience.

Ukrainian Regulations That Guide Online Gambling

In the 1900s, gambling was popular in Ukraine. Then, the country was still a part of Russia. Later in the 1930s, betting houses and activities were closed to keep workers focused. The only places where betting was allowed were Racetracks. After Ukraine's independence in 1990, they opened the first legal casino. This continued for years; the citizens enjoyed gambling freely. Pockets of casinos in big and small sizes opened all over the country until 2009 when the government criminalized gambling.

In this section, we will inform you about gambling law in Ukraine.

Are Online Casinos Legal in Ukraine?

Yes, they are. However, gambling was banned in Ukraine before 2020. 

Before that year, no form of gambling was allowed in Ukraine. The gambling law criminalized the activity in 2009, and every offender had to face the punishment. However, the government changed its mind about this in 2020.

The gambling business is only allowed in authorized hotels in Ukraine. This may be very limited, but it's better compared to the previous position, which was illegal casinos. The government signed the Act into law on August 11, 2020. They also created the Commission for the Regulation of Gambling and Lotteries to oversee the industry. This body issues licenses and monitors the gambling activities of operators.

The law provides basic gambling regulations, licensing taxes, and fees. After the announcement, a lot of international gambling companies applied for the gambling license. These companies can only function within hotels. The gambling license for the Ukrainian body is also quite expensive. It costs 1 million Euros and will be renewed in 5 years. 

What is the Legal Gambling Age in Ukraine?

The legal age to gamble in Ukrainian online casinos is 21 years old.

Am I Required By Law to Pay Taxes on Online Gambling Wins?

Yes, you are. The law on Ukrainian tax also regulates the gambling industry. It provides the following regulations for casino operators and players. 

Casino Operators

Operators of gambling spots pay corporate income tax on their financial profit. They also pay additional income tax on revenue made from gambling activities. Apart from this, the tax is further divided into the types of games offered. The operators are to pay a 10% tax on gross income from slot machines—18% of revenue from betting, casinos, and other activities. The casino pays the 18% revenue tax after the winning amount paid to players is removed. 

Casino Players

On the part of gamblers, the law requires them to pay 18% personal income tax and 1.5% military levy. The casino operator is responsible for withholding the required tax from the amount of winnings and remitting it.
