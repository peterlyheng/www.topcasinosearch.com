Romanian Online Gambling Laws and Regulations

Staying informed about the Romanian gambling market is vital for a smooth online gambling experience in Romania. Here, we break down the basics: the legality of online casinos, the age you must be to gamble, and how winnings are taxed.

Are Online Casinos Legal in Romania?

In Romania, online casino sites are legal, thanks to a framework established in 2015. To operate legally, these sites must obtain a license from the Romanian National Gambling Office (ONJN) and follow set rules. Romania's gambling rules are often seen as the most flexible and accommodating in the EU.

What is the Legal Gambling Age in Romania?

To take part in any form of gambling, including Romania online casinos, you must be 18 years old in Romania.

Does Romania collect taxes on Online casino winnings?

In the past, Romanian gamblers had to pay a 25% tax on their winnings. But things have changed, and now players are taxed at 16% on winnings above 15,000 euros. For those who win €100,000 or more, the 25% tax rate still applies.
