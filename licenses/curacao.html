    What is the Curacao Gambling Commission?

    The Curacao Gambling Commission is a regulatory gambling authority established in 1999 to promote gambling responsibly and modernize gambling legislation. Also referred to as the Curacao eGaming Authority, it was part of the Netherlands Antilles before being dissolved in 2010. Its main function is to issue licenses to online gambling operators. With this license, international casinos can offer various online gambling products, including sports betting, online slots, live casino games, and more.

    The commission also oversees compliance with anti-money laundering (AML) regulations. It requires licensed operators to implement AML procedures to prevent illegal financial activities within their platforms. Overall, the Curacao Gambling Commission is vital for regulating online gambling in Curacao.

    What specific criteria does Curacao use to evaluate the so-called Curacao-certified online casinos?

    The Curacao Gambling Commission uses specific criteria to evaluate and ensure casino websites meet high standards. These include:

    1. Anti-Money Laundering (AML) Procedures Implementation: All Curacoa licensed casinos must have policies, procedures, and technologies to prevent money laundering.
    2. Fair Gaming: The Curacao Gambling Commission is heavy on fair gaming. Game providers must make available responsible gambling tools and make it easy for customers to report and settle disputes.
    3. RNG Certificate: Every Curacoa-certified casino must have a Random Number Generator (RNG) Certificate. It's a software or hardware algorithm that ensures the statistical randomness of numbers. This gives every player a fair shot at winning.
    4. Protection of Minors: Curacoa-certified casinos must commit to not registering minors on their website.
    5. Responsible Gambling Guidelines: To create a safe and secure playing environment for gamers, Curacoa licensed casinos must have procedures in place to address issues like gambling addiction.

    Why do online casinos apply for the Curacao license?

    Gambling sites seek a Curacao license for several key reasons:

    1. Global Reach: The Curacao casino license allows casinos to operate internationally, attracting players from various countries. This broadens their market and enhances their user base.
    2. Financial Operations: Holding a Curacao casino license enables casinos to open a bank account in Curacao, facilitating smooth financial transactions for users.
    3. Reputation: Curacao's endorsement of its gambling license lends credibility within the gambling industry. It is recognized for maintaining a balance between regulation and business freedom.
    4. Regulatory Compliance: To obtain and retain the license, casinos are required to host their servers in Curacao. This ensures compliance with local regulations and contributes to the integrity of their operations.
