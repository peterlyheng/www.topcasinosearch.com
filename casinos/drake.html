Drake Casino has both good and not-so-good reviews from players. Some players enjoy the games and website. In contrast, others face issues, especially with payments and customer service. The user interface made the website easy to navigate, as we could quickly identify and understand every detail and item on the page.

We were greeted with a massive welcome bonus at the top of the screen, with a “Join Now” button right below it, and at the top right corner of the screen, along with a “Login” button. A menu at the top of the page featured “Games,” “Promotions,” “Cashier,” and “Rewards.” 

You will notice a menu button at the top right corner of the page. Clicking the menu button displays a sidebar allowing you to access different website sections. You can access the ‘Games,’ ‘Tournaments,’ ‘Join Now,’ ‘Log In,’ ‘Player Journey,’ ‘Drake Club’ and ‘Contact Us’ pages.

There are a few jackpot options on the home page. You can also see featured games, slots, table games, and live casino games.

At the bottom of the home page, we found shortcuts to different sections of the website, logos of the payment methods available, and secure gaming certifications.

Closing Thoughts

Overall, Drake Casino, like others, has its pros and cons. They have a mixed reputation among their users, with some enjoying the games and others having issues with payment and support.

We like that they offer many games that have been well categorized with filters for ease of navigation. Their customer support is also very helpful and has a quick response time. They have great promotions, and their terms are clear and fair. The KYC process is also straightforward. They accept many payment methods, which makes it convenient for many players to find their preferred option.

On the other hand, we did not find Drake’s licensing information on the website, which raises some alarms. Also, we did not like that the game providers were not mentioned. Drake accepts payments only in USD, which would be stressful for players who use another currency and would have to go through the hassle of converting to USD before they can make payments and also to withdraw their winnings.

We recommend weighing the pros and cons before making your final decision on whether to play here or not.
