Weiss Casino Overview

Get ready for an exciting journey as we delve into the world of Weiss Casino. In this review, we'll explore every corner of this online gaming platform to help you make the right choice.

We have done the work to verify their promise of thrilling games, tempting bonuses, and more. So, if you're considering gambling at this online casino, welcome! Join us as we embark on this adventure and find out if it's worth it.

Reputation

Weiss Casino recently launched in 2023, so there is little information on its reputation. So far, we have seen no major complaints, and they have a good overall reputation in the online casino industry.

Design and User Experience

The website's design is simple and utilizes a lot of white space. This feature makes it less distracting and pleasing to the eyes. They also have excellent graphics with high-quality images.

Scrolling down the home page, we noticed that the first section was "promotions," followed by "popular." They also had other sections like "tournaments," "new games," "buy bonus," and others.

The promotions slider featured five promotions, including a welcome bonus package of 300% up to 5000 USDT + 200 Free spins. The Promotions section later on this page provides more details on this bonus and other bonuses.

We found the Weiss casino website easy to navigate as we could locate things seamlessly. A sidebar makes the location of your favorite games and other pages on the website accessible. At the top of the sidebar, you'll find "Casino," "Sport," and "Daily Spin." You can also navigate to "Provider," "Promotions," "Tournaments," and "Loyalty Program" on the sidebar.

When you reach the bottom of the page, you'll find their licensing information, payment methods, terms and conditions, and others. The customer support live chat icon is at the bottom right.

In our opinion, the Weiss Casino website has a great user experience.

Game Selection

Weiss Casino has a wide variety of games supplied by many notable providers. They have over 5300 games, of which 4281 are slot games. This is an impressive number. Their game providers are mentioned on the website, including Evoplay, Gamzix, Oryx Gaming, and Kalamba Games.

We made a checklist of the casino games available;

  • Tournaments - yes. When we wrote this review, two tournaments were running, and seven other tournaments were showing as “Ended.”
  • Live dealer games - yes. They had different variants of live roulette, blackjack, Monopoly, and some game shows.
  • Table games - yes. Different variants of table games like roulette, blackjack, and baccarat were available.
  • Jackpot games - yes. There were 382 jackpot games on the Weiss casino website when we wrote this.
  • Specialty games - yes, Weiss had many specialty games, including Bingo, keno, and craps.

Navigating Through Games and Filters

It's easy to find your favorite games on Weiss' site. The casino games were clearly categorized into game types like "Slots," "Roulette," and "Live dealer games." There is a sidebar where you can choose to view all games or a specific category of games. You can also use the search button if there is a specific game you want to find. And if you prefer games by a specific game provider, there's a filter you can apply that'll show only games by that provider.

Other filters you can apply are "hot," "new," "jackpot," "fruits," "recommended," and others.

Payments and Processing Times

Weiss Casino offers several deposit and withdrawal options, but what you can use depends on your location. Some methods are only for deposits, while others are just for withdrawals.

Most of their payment methods involve cryptocurrencies. They also accept Astropay, Skrill, Visa, MasterCard, and a few others. Internet banking is possible in a few countries like Canada.

We made a 20 USDT deposit, and it was processed right away. Then, we played slots like Big Bass Splash, Wild Bandito, and European Roulette.

Making a withdrawal

We had to meet a 1x wagering requirement to make a withdrawal. Once we completed the requirements, we were asked to wait seven days until we eventually received our funds. 

Customer Support

Weiss casino live chat can be contacted by clicking “Live Support” on the sidebar.

We started by asking a simple question, to which they answered after 5 minutes with an unclear answer. Instead of giving us a straight answer, they referred us to their terms and conditions.

We followed up with another question, and their response showed they were not very knowledgeable. It also seemed a bit curt and rude. Overall, we did not like their live chat agent.

Promotions

The Weiss casino site had a couple of available bonuses, which were featured on the promotions slider on the homepage. However, when we navigated to the promotions page, we only found details on the welcome bonuses and a tokenized rakeback on every bet. These were the bonuses we found:

  • Welcome Bonus: Enjoy a 125% deposit match + 80 free spins on your first deposit. The second deposit is a 100% match + 40 free spins, and a 75% match + 80 free spins for the third deposit. These bonuses have attached terms and conditions, so read carefully before claiming any. 
  • High Roller Bonus: Highrollers can get a 125% deposit match up to 3000 USDT. No further information was provided on this bonus.
  • Cashback Bonus: Weiss offers up to 40% cashback, depending on your loyalty level. 

In reality, there is only one bonus as their token doesn't have a real money value. Also, no further information about the other bonuses featured on the homepage is provided about them anywhere. Clicking on these bonuses led to the deposit page. Please check their promotions page for more information on the welcome bonus terms.

We also noted that clicking on a promotion automatically starts a deposit process without showing all the terms. This is not something we like. We prefer the bonus terms to be clear and right before us.
