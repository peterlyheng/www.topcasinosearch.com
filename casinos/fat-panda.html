Reputation 

Fatpanda is a new casino that has yet to establish a reputation in the online casino world. During our off-site research, we did not find many user reviews.

When we visited the website for an on-site review, no significant red flags stood out. The casino is licensed with fair terms and has okay customer support. Despite this, we suggest approaching with caution if you choose to play here. Of course, we will update this when we get new information on their reputation.

The website’s landing page has a slider at the top displaying a 100% welcome bonus. The welcome bonus offers a 100% match, giving new players up to €500 extra on their first deposit. This offer also comes with free spins that you can use on the designated slot game. We discussed FatPanda’s promotions more later in the review. 

There are two menus at the top of the home page. A menu bar and a button that opens a sidebar. Each of the menus leads to the same pages: “Live Casino,” “Casino,” “Promotions,” “Tournaments,” and “Contact Us.” Scrolling down the home page gives you an idea of the types of games available at FatPanda Online Casino.

When we reached the bottom, we saw the different providers that supply the website’s games. We also saw shortcuts, payment options, and licensing information.
