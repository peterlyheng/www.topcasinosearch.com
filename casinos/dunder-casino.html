Dunder Casino is recognized in the online gaming community for its solid reputation. However, it's worth noting that there have been some complaints regarding unusual behavior in games. These grievances are often attributed to players experiencing a streak of bad luck rather than any inherent issues with the games themselves. While these concerns are likely based on the natural variability of gaming outcomes, they are nonetheless taken into account when assessing the overall reputation of Dunder Casino. Scrolling down, you will see the wagering requirements written in small texts, and right under that, the different payment methods supported on the website are shown by their icons. Scrolling further down, we discovered the notable game providers highlighted on Dunder's home page. These providers have a reputation for creating fair and secure games. A couple of exciting promotions are also available for you to check out.

At the bottom of the home page, we came across shortcuts to the About Dunder, Games, Help, Terms & Conditions, and Affiliates pages. Below the shortcuts is an option to change your region and the Dunder's licensing information.

Customer Support

Dunder's customer support button is a green and white icon at the bottom right of the screen. On clicking on this button, the first thing we saw was an option to search the FAQs. We didn't find the answer to our question, so we typed it out. A chatbot responded but did not have an answer for us. We were then connected to a real person.

We started with an easy question, but the support agent did not have an answer for us in over 10 minutes. This made us remove points because they didn't seem knowledgeable about the casino. However, we liked that they updated us 5 minutes in that they were still checking for the answer. They didn't just leave us waiting around for the answer.

We asked some other questions, none of them technical,  but it was apparent that the support was not very knowledgeable. There was a quick response time to all our other questions, and the support was polite and respectful.

They don’t have mobile support. We also reached out to them via their email address [email protected]. We asked two questions and got a response in about 4 hours. We found these answers more helpful and knowledgeable. In our opinion, you should contact them via email for more technical questions. For basic questions and a quick response time, their live chat is your best bet. 

We also asked the email support about the no-bonus wagering requirement because the answer we got from the live chat didn't seem correct. We were informed that players would have to play through once to withdraw their funds if they don't activate any bonus.
