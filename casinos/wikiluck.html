WikiLuck Casino Overview

Are you searching for a new casino to add to your gaming radar? If WikiLuck casino is one of the casinos youre considering, then youre in the right place.

Our experts have done a deep offsite and onsite review of this casino and brought our findings to you. This new casino has a below-average reputation and unfair terms and conditions. Their customer support is also poor; we tested both email and livechat and detailed our experience in this review. Are these cons a deal breaker for you? Or will the pros more than makeup for them? Keep reading to find out!

Welcome to our WikiLuck Casino Review!

Reputation 

WikiLuck has an overall below-average reputation. Their withdrawal terms are not fair, and we will expand on this later on this page. They have win limits, and dormant accounts could have their accounts confiscated. Due to these, we give them a low reputation score and advise considering other alternatives.

However, it is a new casino, and there is not too much of an online reputation or player reviews to judge by. There is also time to improve, so we will keep watching it closely. 

Design and User Experience

Wiki Luck Casino has a beautiful website. Its predominantly white theme gives the page an attractive layout, allowing it to add other colors without looking too cluttered. The images on the design are also high quality, giving the website an overall elevated look.

The landing page is a huge banner advertising their welcome bonus, a 4-in-1 deposit match package of up to 1000. More details on this bonus and other promotions will follow later on this page. At the top of the page is a menu bar with the brand logo, a flag icon to switch your country, the login, and the Register buttons. The menubar also contains shortcuts to: Home, Casino, Live Casino, TV Games, Virtual Sports, Sports, Live Betting, ESports, and Promotions.

The homepage has no sidebar. We scrolled past this banner to see a section showing their game categories, followed by a few of their recommended casino games. This then led to their sports betting categories and then their game selection. 

After this, we came across their jackpot section, where their jackpot winners and Total Jackpot were displayed. These were followed up with their jackpot games. This led to their live casino section, where we saw many available live dealer games on the Wikiluck website.

Then, we reached the bottom of the page. This portion of the homepage has their accepted payment logos, shortcuts to different pages of their website, and licensing information.

Overall, the WikiLuck website is beautiful and easy to navigate. However, a sidebar would significantly improve users' experience. 

Game Experience

WikiLuck Casino has a large selection of games, including sports betting and TV games. They have a large online slot selection, and we saw titles such as Gate of Olympus, Sweet Bonanza, and Book of The Fallen. The games are supplied by reputable providers, though these are not indicated on each game. Some of these providers are Microgaming, Betsoft, and Evoplay.

Here is a checklist of available games on WikiLucks website:

  • Tournaments - Yes. There were five running tournaments when we wrote this review.
  • Live dealer games - Yes. They have a separate page for their live casino games. Different variants of live roulette, baccarat, and game shows were available.
  • Jackpot games - Yes. WikiLuck has many jackpot game options, including Noble Sky, Treasure Nile, and Fairy Dust.
  • Specialty games - Yes. Specialty games such as Plinko, Dice, Candy Dreams, and Darts were available.

Navigating Through Games and Filters

Navigating to WikiLucks game page is straightforward. You can click Casino on either the menu bar or the bottom of the page. On the game page, theres a menu bar that you can use to switch to the different categories of games available. These are Video Slots, Top Games, New Games, Table Games, Video Poker, Jackpot Games, and Casual Games.

There is a search bar to find games by title. This search feature has no filter, so you have to know the specific title of the game you want to play. 

There are also two filters to aid navigation. One is for the game providers, and the other can be used to sort games by Most Popular or alphabetically.

Customer Support

WikiLuck Casino claims to be available via livechat, email, and phone support. However, we did not find their mobile contact detail on their page. We tested both their live chat and email support, and this section gives a complete description of our experience.

Live Chat

WikiLucks livechat is a tiny black message bubble icon at the bottom right of the page.

When we clicked on the chat icon, we were asked to fill in our details. We did this, and our position on the queue was indicated as 2. After 15 minutes, our position moved up to 1, and then we were connected to an agent within a minute.

The agent asked for our account details, but we informed them that we had yet to register. After this, our question was answered. 

We followed up with a slightly more technical question. The agent asked us to clarify, which we did. The agent was not knowledgeable and once again referred us to the terms and conditions. It was a simple yes or no question that they should have been able to answer clearly.

Overall, our experience with WikiLucks livechat left us less than impressed. It took a long time for us to be attended to, but once they did, the agents responses came quickly. While the agent was polite and professional, their lack of solid knowledge about the casino caused us to deduct part of their score for customer support.

Email

Parting Words

Our review of WikiLuck casino reviewed a few things we liked and others that we did not.

On one hand, they have a wide selection of casino games covering categories such as slots, live dealer games, jackpots, and casual games. They also offer generous bonuses with fair terms. The website design is beautiful and super easy to navigate. Although they lack a sidebar, the website is well organized, so players can easily figure out where things are. The game search bar and filters are also a plus.

On the other hand, they have a below-average reputation due to their unfair terms. These include low withdrawal limits, high no-bonus playthrough requirements, and the confiscation of the funds of dormant accounts.

WikiLuck is a new casino, so there is still much room for improvement. Also, things constantly change in the online gambling scene, so we will keep updating this review.
