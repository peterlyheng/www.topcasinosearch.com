Reputation


JustSpin online casino has an average reputation among online gamblers. This is due to the mixed reviews and feedback from their players. Most of these revolve around challenges players face during the withdrawal process and issues related to the Know Your Customer (KYC) process.

Many users have reported delays with withdrawals and also a lack of communication from the casino. These withdrawal issues have left players feeling frustrated and losing their trust in the reliability of the casino.

Also, some players have described the KYC process at JustSpin Casino as inconsistent and burdensome.

While JustSpin Casino offers a variety of games and promotions that attract players, issues with withdrawal delays and the KYC process have affected its standing in the online gambling community. Resolving these concerns would help improve JustSpin Casino's reputation and provide users with a more reliable and enjoyable experience.

Customer Support

We found JustSpins support page by clicking Contact Us on the sidebar. On this page, we saw three options to contact them. Their email support can be reached at [email protected]. You could also contact them via live chat or by filling out the query form on the page. However, they have no mobile support.

We clicked Live Chat, and a message box popped up. First, we saw an option to search through their FAQs, and this was where we found information on their daily withdrawal limit.

Next, we clicked messages in an attempt to contact the support team via live chat, but we couldnt. We also tried other things but could not find a way to reach the live chat.

After this, we filled out the query form. We got a response to our mail about 15 minutes later. The response email informed us that they could not find an account linked to our mail, so we would not get an answer. From their reply, we inferred that only registered customers can access their live chat. This needs to be improved.

Then, we contacted their email support. The reply came in less than an hour, a good response time. Their answers were knowledgeable and thoroughly answered our questions. Their email support gets a good rating, but the lack of live chat and answers to our query box question made us deduct a few points from their overall customer support rank.

Conclusion

JustSpin casino, like any other online casino, has pros and cons.

They have a wide selection of games, with an impressive online slot collection. These games are easy to browse through with all the navigation tools provided, such as the sidebar, menu bar, search feature, and neat categorization.

On the flip side, JustSpin has an average reputation, and this is due to the long KYC process that has drawn a lot of complaints from players. Also, their customer support is difficult to reach, as we discovered during our review, and this is one of the common complaints by players.

We gave them a low ranking for their payment methods due to their inaccessibility before registering or logging in to this casino site. They also have a lot of restricted countries.

Players can feel a level of safety playing at JustSpin casino due to their MGA license and reputable game providers. However, we advise considering the average reputation and weighing the pros and cons before making your final decision to play here.
