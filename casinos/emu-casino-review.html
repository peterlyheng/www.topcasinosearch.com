EmuCasino Casino Overview

Emu Online Casino was first launched in 2012. You couldn’t tell that they have been around for a while by their sleek, modern-looking website design. They offer a decent selection of games from the biggest software providers, and they have a legal license, so you can feel safe wagering with them to an extent. Other factors, such as the casino’s reputation and game providers, come into play in determining the safety of a casino.

The website is available on both Desktop and Mobile. We did a thorough check of the website and brought you this honest review of their website. Keep reading to find out if they meet your expectations!

Reputation

Emu Casino has gotten mixed reviews from users. While there aren't significant issues, some people mention average service. Most complaints have been about their customer support and the gaming experience, which some find lacking compared to other online casinos. 

Despite offering many games, Emu Casino struggles to meet high expectations for excellent service and quick customer support. This feedback suggests room for improvement to make players happier and compete better in the online gambling world.

Design and User Experience

Emu Casino has a creative website design. When you visit, you see the website’s mascot, the emu, all over the home page.

At the top of the home page, there is a menu bar featuring game tabs that lead you to “All Games,” “Promotions,” “Slots,” “Live Casino,” and “Casino Races.” The “Sign up” and “Log In” buttons are on the right of the menu bar.

There was a huge slider showing Emu’s promotions. The slider switched between their no-deposit 12 free spins offer you get as soon as you sign up, their $300 triple boost welcome bonus, and jackpots. More information on these bonuses will be provided in the Promotions section further down this page.

On scrolling down, we saw a few games from some categories, giving you a sneak peek at what Emu Casino offers. 

Near the bottom of the home page was an article about Emu Casino and this was followed by some FAQs you can check out.

At the bottom of the page are links to Emu’s social platforms, shortcuts to pages on the website, logos of Emu’s game providers, and the payment options available. 

Game Selection

Emu Casino features over 2,100 games ranging from slots to live casino games and virtual races. They are supplied by some of the most famous game providers, including Microgaming, Betsoft, BGaming, Pragmatic Play, NetEnt, etc.

Navigating Through Games and Filters

You get a sneak peek of Emu’s casino game catalog on the home page. You can easily access the casino hub from the menu bar at the top of the home page. Click on All Games to see all of Emu’s games, and click on any category of yours to narrow your view to the games in that category alone.

Customer Support

At the time of this review, we could not contact a support agent via live chat because no agent was available. 

Since we could not speak with a support agent directly, we typed our questions in the message box. 

We received an email informing us that a ticket had been opened for us immediately after filling the query box. The actual response came in about 50 minutes later.

We were informed that they did not accept players from the country we asked about. Our second question was not answered.

The “Customer support” page states that they offer email support but does not include the email address. And there was no indication of phone support.

Terms

Emu Casino’s terms and conditions are neatly itemized, each with clear headings to make them easy to find. They were also comprehensive; we found all the essential information we needed.

We checked the bonus terms and requirements to ensure they were transparent and fair to players. 

Below, we outlined the wagering requirements, withdrawal limits, and KYC procedure:

Emu Casino’s withdrawal limits

Emu Casino has a monthly withdrawal limit of $20,000 for all customers. We consider this withdrawal limit as too low. It would be difficult for players with huge cashouts to get all their money out. The best limit is either between $60,000 and $100,000 or no limit at all. No daily or weekly withdrawal limits were stated.

Emu Online Casino’s wagering requirements

  • The wagering requirement for a no-bonus withdrawal is 1x.
  • The wagering requirements for bonus withdrawals are 30x - 45x.

Emu Online Casino’s KYC process

Emu Casino’s KYC process involves:

  • A photo of your driver's license or another government-issued ID.
  • A selfie, which might include you holding certain documents for identification.
  • Proof of your address. They could either request a utility bill or a bank statement.
  • Evidence of your financial account for deposits and withdrawals.

Sometimes, they might request notarized copies of these documents for added verification.

Conclusion

Emu Casino presents itself as a well-established online gaming platform with its sleek and modern design, game catalog ranging from slots to live dealer games and valuable promotions.

Another exciting thing about Emu Casino is that they have a YouTube platform where they post helpful video guides that they link to the website. They also have clear gambling rules, terms, and limits, with various cash-out payment options. You can find some peace of mind in knowing that Emu Casino is licensed. Factors such as Emu Casino’s reputation and the game providers should be considered before concluding if it's safe to play there.

However, there were certain things we did not like. First, the monthly withdrawal limit of $20,000 is too low. Also, we could not access customer support via live chat. Their email address was not provided, and there was no mobile number. The response to our questions via the query box was fast, but our second question was unanswered.

Bear in mind that our review is solely based on our experience with the website. We encourage you to make personal findings and weigh all the pros and cons before deciding to play here. Stay safe, and have fun in your online gambling journey!
