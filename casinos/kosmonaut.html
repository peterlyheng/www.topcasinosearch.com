Kosmonaut Casino Overview

Picture an exciting journey through the cosmos of online gaming. That's what Kosmonaut Casino offers. In this review, we'll take you on an honest tour of this casino. We'll show you what we love and don't love about this site. Let's dive into this cosmic adventure and discover if it should be on your gaming radar.

Reputation 

Kosmonaut Casino seems to have a mixed bag when it comes to reputation. On one hand, some users report having a good experience, praising aspects like their game selection and user interface. However, there's also a fair share of less satisfied customers who have raised concerns over issues like customer service or withdrawal processes. 

Given these contrasting user reviews and our own assessment, it's a bit of a grey area. To be honest, with the online feedback not being straightforward, we can't really place Kosmonaut Casino among the top tier of online casinos in terms of reputation. It's not that they're terrible, but they don't quite hit the mark of what we'd consider a highly reputable brand.

Design and User Experience

Kosmonaut Casino has a crisp and classy design. The theme is predominantly black, which makes it easy to add all the other colors of the casino without any clashes. The overall design gives the website a beautiful look and makes it inviting to players.

Upon landing on the page, you're greeted with a visually stunning banner showcasing their generous welcome bonus- a 300% deposit bonus up to 5000 and 350 free spins. We've thoroughly reviewed this bonus and all others, and our findings are detailed in the Promotions section below.

At the very top of the page is a menu bar with an icon that you can use to download their app, a search bar, the Login and Signup buttons, and also a flag icon to switch your country.

The sidebar did not contain as many items as we would have expected. It contained only a few items; “TOURNAMENTS,” “BONUSES,” “LOYALTY,” “PAYMENTS,” and “INSTANT VIP.”

Scrolling down the page led us through a section of their games and promotions. The games were grouped into different categories, such as “Top Games,” “Highest payout slots,” and “Live.”

We also came across their total jackpot amount won, game providers, tournaments, and players' reviews. After these, we reached the bottom of the page, which contained shortcuts to other pages on the website. It also included the logos of their payment methods, some of their certifications, and their licensing information. Navigating the Kosmonaut website was a breeze and we had a great user experience.

Game Selection

Kosmonaut has 6492 casino games, of which 5544 are slots. This is an impressive collection of games. These game providers are displayed on them, which is a nice touch. Some of the providers include Bgaming, Playngo, PragmaticPlay, and Netgame.

  • Specialty games - Yes, Kosmonaut also had many specialty games, covering bingo, keno, and craps.

Navigating Through Games and Filters

Getting to Kosmonaut’s game page is straightforward. Simply click on “View all” on the homepage. The page has all the casino games neatly categorized, with the number of each category included in a menu bar. One of our favorite things about this games section was their separate category of Bitcoin games. This makes it easy for players who prefer to pay with crypto to find the games they can play.

There is a search bar to find games by name. This search also has two filters: Providers and Categories. This feature makes the navigation of Kosmonaut casino games even more seamless, and it impressed us.

Customer Support

At Kosmonaut Casino, you're never alone on your cosmic gaming journey. They offer a live chat feature that allows you to connect directly with their support agents. The best part? It's not a chatbot – you're in direct contact with real humans. We found this to be a great advantage.

We asked the agent a simple question, which they responded quickly to. We then followed up with questions that were a bit more technical. During our experience, the support agent was knowledgeable. They also provided quick, easy-to-understand answers to all our inquiries. We loved that the live chat was available without registering or logging in.

In their terms, we found their email support [email protected]. However, when we sent an email, the address was not found. Then, we asked the livechat about this and were informed that they have no email support. They also don’t provide phone support.

Payments and Processing Times

You can't view the deposit options at Kosmonaut until you sign up and log in. They mainly use cryptocurrencies but accept Visa, MasterCard, Paysafe cards, and Skrill.

We made a 20 USDT deposit, and the processing time was instant. We had a blast playing slots like "Book of the Dead" and "Lost Vegas." We also played European Roulette.

Making a withdrawal

Our withdrawal was processed instantly. However, we did not receive a payment for a while until an email arrived. The email was an update that we must meet the 3x wagering requirement, as written in the bonus terms. We were aware of this term, so it didn't come as a surprise. We did hope, though, that it didn't apply to every withdrawal.

After we met the 3x wagering requirement, we were asked to go through the KYC process and receive our funds. We deducted many points for this because the terms said you only need this check for withdrawals over $1000, and our withdrawal was less than that.
