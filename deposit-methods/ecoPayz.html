The History of Payz

The company behind Payz is PSI-Pay Ltd. The company was established in 2008 by the FSA (Financial Services Authority) in the United Kingdom. The payment service became popular quickly and, within a few years, gathered a large user base. Soon, they became associated with Mastercard, which recognized the growing popularity. They launched the first-ever financial institution app, with their former name EcoCard.
The payment app gathered traction, and its growing popularity enabled the company to expand its services. EcoCard was replaced with EcoPayz in 2013, resulting in the new branding that was common up to lately. EcoPayz recently changed its name again to Payz. 
Since 2013, they expanded their services and provided payment solutions worldwide. They led a multilingual approach and turned to many audiences. For example, they localized their services for the Indian market. Soon later, they expanded their services as a part of a business arrangement with Western Union. 
Payz is not only an e-wallet. Rather, they also offer a prepaid card called ecoCard, virtual payments using the ecoVirtualCard, and ecoVouchers. These are all ways for users to make easy payments on various platforms. They also offer customers a partnership program, allowing them to profit from bringing new users abroad.

The customer support department is one of the things you can't compromise on. They must be available at all times, friendly, knowledgeable, and ready to assist with any inquiry. And also, it helps if there is more than one way to reach them.

Licensing

Online casinos must have a license to operate and process payments. To find the license number associated with a casino, look at the casino site's footer.
