    VIP Preferred Casino Sites Explained

    VIP-preferred casinos are online casinos that offer the VIP-preferred payment method. VIP preferred is a digital payment method that uses ACH transfers or eChecks. You can use it for deposits and withdrawals at online casinos. To use this method, you have to link your bank details (like your bank routing number), but you only have to do this once, as it will be saved.

    VIP preferred also uses eChecks, which is usable at land-based casinos. eChecks are like cashing a check at the bank but less problematic. VIP Preferred uses your ID or VIP Preferred card to authorize your transaction. The payment option's design was tailored specifically for casinos. This makes it safe and hassle-free. VIP preferred only works with licensed and regulated casinos, so you won't find this payment option at fraudulent casino sites.

    History of VIP Preferred

    The history of VIP preferred dates back to 1967. George Thorpe was a retired US Air Force director who specialized in data processing systems and became part of the National Data Corporation. He created Global Payments Inc., which later separated from its parent company in 1966. However, despite the risky financial climate in the mid-1990s, the separation paid off.

    Global Payments Inc. was publicly traded on the New York Stock Exchange and is currently worth billions of dollars. Due to the legalization of gambling in the US, the VPN preferred was launched in 2012. Although the number of sites that use this payment method is few, more sites will start to use it with time.

    Here is a step-by-step process for withdrawing into your VIP Preferred account.
