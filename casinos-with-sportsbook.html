    Different Types of Sports Betting

    There is an extensive selection for sports fans who enjoy betting to choose from. They include:

    Moneyline betting:

    This is the most common form of betting. Here, you choose the team or the individual in the game that you think will be the winner. The sportsbook assigns odds to each team/individual in the game.

    Point spread betting:

    In this type of betting, the points between the favorite team/player and the underdog are equal. The sportsbook assigns fewer points than deserved to the favorite and assigns the underdog an equal number. Bettors are then required to bet on the spread, whether the favorite will win by more points or if the underdog will lose by fewer points.

    Parlay Betting:

    This happens when a player combines all their bets into one wager. The advantage of this type of bet is that the payout is usually larger than when players bet differently. Although the risks are higher, all the bets in the parlay must be successful for the player to win.

    Prop Betting:

    This is also known as proposition betting. It is the type of betting done when a player predicts specific outcomes of certain parts of the game. Examples include the number of goals/points scored by a player or the direction of a coin toss.

    Futures Betting:

    The odds of this type of betting are uncertain as they change over time. The game involves a player betting on the outcome of an event, for example, who will win a championship.
